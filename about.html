About BWM Machinery

B.W.Mack (Machinery) Ltd has been serving the farming community in Downham Market, Norfolk for many years. Our dedicated team spans four key departments, providing comprehensive support and service to every customer.

Admin & Accounts

Handling all financial and administrative operations, ensuring smooth day-to-day running of the business and prompt invoicing and customer support.

Sales

Expert guidance across our full range of new and used machinery. Our team works with you to find the right equipment for your operation and budget.

Service

Factory-trained technicians delivering manufacturer-standard maintenance, diagnostics, and repairs to keep your machinery performing at its best.

Stores & Parts

A comprehensive parts inventory covering all represented brands, ensuring fast fulfilment and minimal machine downtime throughout the season.
